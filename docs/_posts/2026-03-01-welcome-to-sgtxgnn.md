# Welcome to SgTxGNN

We're excited to launch **SgTxGNN**, a drug repurposing prediction platform specifically designed for Singapore's healthcare ecosystem.

## What is SgTxGNN?

SgTxGNN uses Harvard's TxGNN model to predict potential new uses for drugs already approved by Singapore's Health Sciences Authority (HSA). Our platform provides:

- **31,543 predictions** across 745 drugs
- **Dual validation** using both Knowledge Graph and Deep Learning methods
- **FHIR R4 API** for integration with healthcare systems
- **SMART on FHIR apps** for EHR integration

## Why Drug Repurposing?

Traditional drug development takes 10-15 years and costs over $1 billion. Drug repurposing offers a faster, more cost-effective alternative by finding new uses for existing medications that already have established safety profiles.
